I am an evolutional and developmental biologist, interested in how genomic evolution impacts developmental processes that contribute to morphological diversity.

I completed my honours degree in biology at Oxford Brookes University in 2013. I received my PhD from Oxford Brookes University in 2018, working with Alistair McGregor. I then moved to University College London for postdoctoral research with Max Telford.

In 2020 I moved to Durham University as a postdoctual researcher and also became an Associate Lecturer at Oxford Brookes University.

You can find out about my research projects and publications. Also, feel free to contact") me.
